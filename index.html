Classic cheesecake

Description

This is the simplest and most old school recipe of cheesecake. Everyone loves cheesecake so having the skill of creating one from scratch is a good one to have.

Ingredient list

    2 pounds of cream cheese 1 cup sugar 1 cup sour cream 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract 2 teaspoons lemon juice 3 eggs

Steps

More detailed instructions are on the recipes link

    Preheat oven to 170C (350F) Make the crust Make the filling Prepare the water bath Bake the cake for 55-70 minutes Cool the cake down per the instructions the link Slice it up, add toppings of choice
